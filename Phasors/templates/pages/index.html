Hello There

We Are Phasors

Phasors, a platform where students from various IITs and NITs,join together to help the upcoming JEE aspirants reach their goals and make their path smooth and easy. Phasors also help enlighten the obscured path of these aspirants.

Afterall, a light of hope emerges from the tales of those who succeeded.Unlike any other portal or platform, Phasors aims to deliver an all round guidance to these aspirants. Phasors believes that the journey of these aspirants is entirely for one's own dreamland, one's own target. Phasors is the brainchild of those people who fancy helping and passing on knowledge

What We Do

We are hear to pass knowledge to rural and other people

Phasors does not believe in White Noise guidance. We only believe in the idea of one tune that resonated with one's dream. At phasors, we make customised guidance for each aspirant. And all this is purely out of our experience. We heart fully appreciate the people who offered their helping hand and are very passionate towards passing on the knowledge and help our juniors.

If you think why we're doing this customised help, well the answer is easy. It's ofcourse the passion and yeah, who doesn't want to be a hero ;). At phasors, we understand an aspirant, his problem and offer solution and guidance to his problem. Our online portal is always open and welcomes any kind of queries, technical or non technical
